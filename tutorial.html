Tutorial

Double-click the Xeno download link on our website main page, paste it into a new browser tab, then download and open it

Once you’ve downloaded it, it should look like this

Now go to Settings in the bottom-left corner, then turn on 'Auto Attach'

After enabling Auto Attach, go to Executor

Paste a script from this website or from anywhere else like Scriptblox

You can also right-click the Scripts tab to rename it

After completing those steps, go to Client Manager, where you will see your Roblox username and the game you are currently playing. If you do not see your username, that means Roblox is not running. If it still doesn’t appear, try restarting Xeno

Look at the top-right of your Client Manager page. If it says 0 of 1 selected, click on your username/bar and it should then display 1 of 1 selected

After this, you’re all set! Go back to the Executor and click Execute to run your scripts. Enjoy exploiting!
